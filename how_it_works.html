How FitDoctor Virtual Try-On Works

1. Upload Your Photos

Start by uploading a clear, full-body photo of yourself along with an image of the clothing item you want to try on. For best results, ensure your photo has good lighting and the clothing image has a plain background.

2. AI Processing

Our advanced AI algorithms analyze both images to blend the clothing realistically onto your photo. This process takes just a few moments.

3. View & Download

Once processed, you’ll see the virtual try-on result instantly. You can then download the image to share or save your new look.

Why Choose FitDoctor?

  • Privacy-first approach — your photos stay on your device unless you choose to share.
  • Fast and lightweight, no heavy software required.
  • Realistic, high-quality virtual try-ons.
